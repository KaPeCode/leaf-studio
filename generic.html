Our story

We are Aleksandra and Artur, the team behind Leaf Studio.
Leaf Studio grew out of shared passion for homes – not just how they look, but how they work. We love to work together and bring our different strengths to every project – technical knowledge, creative vision, and attention to detail.

Find a registered assessor

Artur, a skilled electrician with over 15 years of industry experience and is also a fully accredited EPC and Retrofit Assessor. Aleksandra brings more than 13 years of expertise in property photography and floor planning and has since expanded her skills to include EPC and retrofit assessments. At Leaf Studio, we are passionate about doing things properly. We pride ourselves on being reliable, accurate, and always focused on what our clients need. Whether providing energy efficiency or retrofit assessments, creating detailed floor plans or stunning property visuals, we are here to help. When we are not working, you’ll likely find us in nature, practising Tai Chi, playing music, tinkering, or baking cakes.
